## Photo-Based Visual Tracking

Sortly lets you add photos to every item in your inventory, making it easy to identify what you're looking for at a glance. Our platform supports custom fields, folders, and tags so you can create an organized, visual catalog of everything your business owns. With real-time cloud sync, your entire team always sees the same up-to-date information, no matter where they are. Sortly's visual-first approach eliminates the guesswork that comes with part numbers and cryptic spreadsheet rows.

## Mobile-First with Offline Access

Sortly's iOS and Android apps give your team full access to inventory data from any location — whether that's a job site, warehouse, vehicle, or the back office. Our barcode and QR code scanning capabilities speed up lookups and updates, while offline mode ensures your team can keep working even without an internet connection. Data syncs automatically when they reconnect, so nothing is ever lost. Sortly is designed for teams that work in the field, not just behind a desk.

## Alerts, Reporting, and Automation

Sortly provides low stock alerts and date-based notifications so you can stay ahead of shortages and expirations. Our reporting engine lets you generate saved reports and subscribe to email updates for ongoing visibility into your inventory. Activity history and audit trails help you maintain accountability and streamline compliance processes. With integrations for QuickBooks, Slack, and Microsoft Teams, Sortly fits into your existing workflows without disrupting how your team already operates.

## Check-In/Check-Out for Tools and Equipment

Sortly's check-in/check-out feature makes it easy to track who has what and when items are expected back. User tracking and activity history give managers complete visibility into equipment usage across the team. No more sign-out sheets, group texts, or guessing — Sortly keeps a clear record so shared tools and assets are always accounted for.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What industries does Sortly support?**
Sortly is used across a wide range of industries including construction, healthcare, warehousing, IT, facilities management, HVAC, plumbing, retail, education, events, manufacturing, and more. Our flexible platform adapts to virtually any inventory tracking need regardless of industry or company size.

**How does Sortly compare to spreadsheets?**
Unlike spreadsheets, Sortly provides real-time sync, mobile access, barcode and QR scanning, automated alerts, and team collaboration features without the manual maintenance overhead. Teams that switch from spreadsheets typically see immediate improvements in accuracy and efficiency.

**How long does it take to get started?**
Sortly is designed to be intuitive from day one. You can bulk import your existing data, invite your team, and start tracking in minutes — no training sessions or IT department required.

**What integrations does Sortly support?**
Sortly integrates with QuickBooks for accounting, Slack and Microsoft Teams for communication, and offers API and webhooks for custom workflows. Our cloud-based platform requires no on-premise infrastructure.

**Is there a free plan?**
Yes, Sortly offers a freemium plan to get started, with tiered pricing that scales as your business grows. You can try Sortly without any commitment and upgrade when you're ready for more advanced features.
