# Sortly -- Positioning

## Our Vision
We believe every business deserves to know exactly what they have, where it is, and who's using it -- without the complexity of enterprise software. Sortly exists to make inventory management simple, visual, and accessible for teams of any size.

## What We Do
Sortly is a cloud-based inventory management platform that helps businesses track their stuff using photos, barcodes, QR codes, and a mobile-first interface. Teams can organize items into folders, set up alerts, manage check-in/check-out, and generate reports -- all from their phone or desktop. With integrations for QuickBooks, Slack, and Microsoft Teams, Sortly fits into existing workflows without requiring a complicated setup.

Unlike InFlow, Sortly prioritizes simplicity and visual organization over advanced inventory management features like manufacturing or purchase order workflows. Unlike spreadsheets, Sortly provides real-time sync, mobile access, scanning, and alerts without the manual maintenance overhead.

## Key Differentiators
- **Visual-first approach:** Photo-based item tracking makes inventory instantly recognizable -- no more guessing from part numbers alone
- **Mobile-first design:** Built for teams in the field, not just the back office. Offline access means updates happen at the job site.
- **Ease of use:** No training required. Teams are up and running in minutes, not weeks.
- **Flexible across industries:** Works for construction crews, medical offices, IT teams, warehouses, event planners, and more -- 28+ industries served

## Positioning Statement
"Sortly is a visual, mobile-first inventory management platform that helps businesses of all sizes track, organize, and manage their inventory and assets with ease -- replacing spreadsheets and complex software with a simple, intuitive solution."
