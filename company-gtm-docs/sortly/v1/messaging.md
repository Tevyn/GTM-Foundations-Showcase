# Sortly -- Messaging Guide

## Elevator Pitch
"Sortly is a visual, mobile-first inventory management app that helps businesses track and organize everything they own -- from supplies and tools to equipment and assets -- using photos, barcodes, and QR codes. It's the simple alternative to spreadsheets and complex inventory software."

## Value Propositions

### See Everything, Instantly
**Headline:** Visual inventory tracking that makes every item instantly recognizable.
**Supporting Copy:** Sortly lets you add photos to every item in your inventory, so your team can identify what they're looking for at a glance -- no more squinting at part numbers or cryptic spreadsheet rows. Combine photos with custom fields, folders, and tags to create an organized, visual catalog of everything your business owns. With real-time cloud sync, everyone on the team sees the same up-to-date information.

### Track From Anywhere
**Headline:** A mobile-first inventory app built for teams in the field.
**Supporting Copy:** Sortly's iOS and Android apps give your team full access to inventory data from job sites, warehouses, vehicles, or anywhere work happens. Barcode and QR code scanning speeds up lookups and updates. Offline mode means your team can keep working even without cell service -- data syncs automatically when they reconnect.

### Get Set Up in Minutes, Not Weeks
**Headline:** The inventory solution your whole team will actually use.
**Supporting Copy:** Sortly is designed to be intuitive from day one. Bulk import your existing data, invite your team, and start tracking -- no training sessions, no IT department required. With a freemium plan to get started and tiered pricing as you grow, Sortly scales with your business without the overhead of enterprise software.

## Brand Voice
- **Simple** -- We keep things clear and jargon-free
- **Friendly** -- We're approachable and helpful, not corporate
- **Confident** -- We know our product makes inventory easier
