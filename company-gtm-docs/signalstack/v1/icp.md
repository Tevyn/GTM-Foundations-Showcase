# SignalStack — Ideal Customer Profile

## Target Market
**Industries:** Technology/Software, Financial Services, SaaS, Cloud-Native Companies
**Company Size:** 50-5,000+ employees; $10M-$500M+ revenue
**Geography:** North America, EMEA, growing in APAC

## Technographics
Currently using one or more monitoring tools (Datadog, New Relic, AWS CloudWatch, Grafana) or manual alert management processes. Often have fragmented observability stacks with multiple data sources and inconsistent alerting logic.

## Buying Triggers
- Scaling engineering operations and incident volume increasing
- High alert fatigue impacting team efficiency
- Looking to improve incident response time
- Recently implementing or expanding monitoring infrastructure
- Team needs better incident collaboration and on-call scheduling

## Buyer Roles
Engineering leaders including VP of Engineering, Director of Platform Engineering, Director of Infrastructure, or Engineering Managers responsible for observability and incident response processes.

## Pain Points
- **Alert noise and fatigue:** Engineering teams receive excessive alerts, leading to alert desensitization and missed critical incidents
- **Slow incident response:** Manual routing and coordination delay response times, increasing incident impact and customer downtime
- **Fragmented tooling:** Multiple monitoring tools across the organization create integration overhead and inconsistent alerting
- **Manual incident workflows:** On-call scheduling, alert routing, and incident timeline reconstruction require manual effort and tools like PagerDuty/OpsGenie integration
- **Visibility gaps:** Cross-service dependencies and alert relationships are unclear, making root cause analysis time-consuming
- **Alert management overhead:** Tuning and managing alert rules across platforms is labor-intensive and never-ending

## Not a Fit
- Companies without active observability/monitoring infrastructure (too early stage)
- Organizations that prioritize build-over-buy and prefer home-grown solutions exclusively
- Enterprises with fully committed Datadog or PagerDuty stacks with no integration needs
