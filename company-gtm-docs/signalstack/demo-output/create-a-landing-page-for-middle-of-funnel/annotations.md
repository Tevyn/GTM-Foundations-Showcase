# Annotations: Create a landing page for middle of funnel

---

> [!v1-passage]
> SignalStack's ML engine learns your alert environment and automatically filters out false positives, duplicate alerts, and low-priority noise. Our pattern recognition technology reduces alert volume by 40-60%, ensuring your team focuses on the incidents that actually matter.

> [!v2-passage]
> You added Datadog for APM. CloudWatch for infrastructure. Prometheus for Kubernetes. Each tool solved a real problem when you brought it in. But now they fire independently — the same failure triggers alerts in three places at once, and your on-call engineer has no idea which one is the root cause and which are symptoms.

**Section:** Page Opening

**V1:** The original ICP is broad, so the page leads with product capabilities and technical claims rather than a situation the buyer recognizes.

**V2:** The focused ICP targets one buyer type, so the page opens inside a scenario they are already living through every week.

**Impact:** Pages that mirror the buyer's actual situation generate higher engagement and more demo requests from qualified prospects.

---

> [!v1-passage]
> SignalStack is an intelligent alert management platform that sits between your monitoring tools and incident management systems. We provide ML-powered noise reduction, smart alert routing, cross-tool correlation, and automated alert tuning to help engineering teams cut through the noise and respond faster to real incidents.

> [!v2-passage]
> Your team gets 50-100+ alerts per day. They've stopped trusting their own pagers. Last month, a critical database incident was buried in 40 duplicates and 30 false positives. By the time someone noticed, the customer had been down for 35 minutes. SLA breach. Angry account team. Renewal at risk.

**Section:** Problem Framing

**V1:** The problem understanding is shallow, so the page describes what the product does instead of what the buyer is losing.

**V2:** The problem understanding is specific and quantified, so the page connects alert noise to missed incidents, SLA breaches, and renewal risk.

**Impact:** Tying product value to customer retention and revenue loss gives the buyer a business case they can bring to their executive team.

---

> [!v1-passage]
> SignalStack is designed for drop-in deployment alongside your existing tools. Most organizations are fully operational within days, not months. There's no need to rip and replace your current monitoring or incident management infrastructure.

> [!v2-passage]
> The typical alternative — consolidating onto a single platform — is a 6-12 month project you can't prioritize right now. Tuning harder is a treadmill that stops working past 1,000 alert rules. SignalStack is the layer that should have existed between your monitoring and your incident response from the beginning.

**Section:** Competitive Framing

**V1:** The original positioning lacks a competitive frame, so the page makes a generic deployment claim without addressing what the buyer has already considered.

**V2:** The focused positioning names the alternatives buyers are weighing, so the page disqualifies those options before the buyer raises them in a sales call.

**Impact:** Handling objections on the landing page shortens sales cycles by moving buyers past the comparison-shopping phase before they ever engage with a rep.
