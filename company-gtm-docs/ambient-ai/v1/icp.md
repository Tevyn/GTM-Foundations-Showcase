# Ambient.ai -- Ideal Customer Profile

## Target Market
**Industries:** Education, Manufacturing, Energy & Utilities, Financial Services, Healthcare, Corporate Campuses, Data Centers, Museums
**Company Size:** 500-50,000+ employees; $100M-$10B+ revenue
**Geography:** North America, EMEA, APAC

## Technographics
Currently operating a video management system (Genetec, Milestone, Avigilon) or standalone camera infrastructure (Hikvision, Bosch, Honeywell, Samsung, Hanwha) with physical access control systems (Allegion, Software House, Brivo). May also be running a Global Security Operations Center (GSOC) with manual monitoring workflows and legacy alarm management tools. Organizations using spreadsheets or incident logging software for forensic investigation and post-incident reporting.

## Buying Triggers
- Looking to modernize physical security operations
- Need to reduce false alarm volume and improve response times
- Seeking to improve efficiency across security teams
- Evaluating AI-driven approaches for threat detection
- Under pressure to do more with existing headcount
- Looking to consolidate security tools and reduce operational complexity
- Need to demonstrate ROI on existing camera infrastructure investments

## Buyer Roles
Directors, VPs, and C-level executives in Physical Security, Corporate Security, Facilities, and Risk Management. Also involves IT leaders (CIO/CISO) when the platform requires cloud infrastructure or network integration. In some cases, campus safety directors (Education) or operations leaders (Manufacturing, Energy) are involved. Security Operations Center managers and GSOC supervisors who influence the evaluation.

## Pain Points
- Security teams are overwhelmed by false alarms and alert fatigue, making it hard to focus on real threats
- Forensic investigations take too long -- manually scrubbing through hours of video footage after incidents
- Guards watching banks of monitors miss critical events because human attention spans are limited
- Existing VMS platforms provide video storage but no intelligence layer for proactive threat detection
- Access control and video systems operate in silos, preventing correlation of events across systems
- Difficulty proving ROI of security programs and justifying headcount to executive leadership
- Compliance and audit requirements are becoming more complex, but security documentation is still manual
- Legacy systems require expensive rip-and-replace upgrades to add any new capability
- Scaling security operations across multiple sites without proportionally increasing staff

## Not a Fit
- Organizations with fewer than 50 cameras or a single small facility (insufficient scale for AI value)
- Companies requiring on-premises-only deployment with no cloud connectivity
- Organizations that have not yet deployed IP cameras (still running analog CCTV)
- Prospects in regions where GDPR or data privacy requirements exceed current certification scope
