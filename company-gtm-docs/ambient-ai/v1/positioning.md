# Ambient.ai -- Positioning

## Our Vision
We believe physical security should be intelligent, not just recorded. Ambient.ai exists to transform how organizations protect their people and assets by bringing AI-powered intelligence to every camera and access point in their environment.

## What We Do
Ambient.ai is an AI-powered physical security intelligence platform that layers computer vision and threat detection onto existing camera and access control infrastructure. The platform continuously monitors video feeds, correlates events across systems, and automates threat detection and investigation -- replacing the legacy model of guards watching monitors with always-on AI intelligence. Cloud-delivered with no rip-and-replace required.

Unlike legacy VMS platforms like Genetec and Milestone, Ambient.ai adds an intelligence layer that transforms cameras from passive recording devices into active threat detection sensors -- without requiring organizations to replace their existing infrastructure. Unlike AI-native startups like Verkada and Rhombus, Ambient.ai is camera-agnostic and works with the cameras and access control systems already deployed, avoiding vendor lock-in and proprietary hardware requirements.

## Key Differentiators
- **AI intelligence overlay, not rip-and-replace:** Deploys on top of existing camera and access control infrastructure, delivering AI capabilities without hardware upgrades
- **95% false alarm reduction:** Access Intelligence correlates video and access control data to eliminate nuisance alarms and surface only verified threats
- **150+ verified threat signatures:** Purpose-built computer vision models trained on real-world physical security scenarios, not repurposed general AI
- **Edge-optimized VLM (Pulsar):** Vision-Language Model designed for physical security use cases with edge deployment for low-latency inference
- **Enterprise-grade security and compliance:** GDPR and SOC 2 Type II certified, trusted by Adobe, TikTok, Cisco, and other Fortune 500 enterprises
