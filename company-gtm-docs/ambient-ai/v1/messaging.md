# Ambient.ai -- Messaging Guide

## Elevator Pitch
"Ambient.ai is an AI-powered physical security intelligence platform that transforms existing camera and access control infrastructure into an intelligent threat detection and response system for enterprise security teams."

## Value Propositions

### Eliminate Alert Fatigue with AI-Powered Alarm Verification
**Headline:** Reduce false alarms by 95% with intelligent video-access correlation
**Supporting Copy:** Ambient.ai's Access Intelligence automatically correlates access control events with real-time video analysis, distinguishing genuine security threats from nuisance alarms. Instead of operators manually triaging hundreds of door-held and badge-violation alerts per shift, the platform verifies each alarm using computer vision -- surfacing only the events that require human attention. Security teams stay focused on real threats instead of drowning in noise.

### Investigate Incidents in Minutes, Not Hours
**Headline:** AI-powered forensic search across all your cameras
**Supporting Copy:** Advanced Forensics lets investigators search video footage by person appearance, object, or activity type across any camera and time range. No more manually scrubbing through hours of footage to reconstruct an incident timeline. What used to take a full shift now takes minutes.

### Detect Threats Before They Escalate
**Headline:** 150+ verified threat signatures for continuous, automated monitoring
**Supporting Copy:** Ambient.ai's Threat Detection uses purpose-built computer vision models to identify weapons, aggressive behavior, perimeter breaches, loitering, and other security events in real time. The platform monitors continuously without the attention fatigue that limits human guards, ensuring critical events are detected and escalated immediately.

### Deploy AI Without Replacing Your Infrastructure
**Headline:** Works with your existing cameras and access control systems
**Supporting Copy:** Ambient.ai integrates with cameras from Hikvision, Bosch, Honeywell, Samsung, and Hanwha, and access control platforms including Genetec, Allegion, Software House, Avigilon, and Brivo. Cloud-delivered with edge processing via Pulsar VLM. No rip-and-replace, no proprietary hardware requirements.

## Brand Voice
- **Authoritative** -- We understand physical security operations at a deep technical and operational level
- **Practical** -- We lead with outcomes and operational impact, not hype or abstract AI promises
- **Confident** -- We back claims with specific metrics and enterprise customer evidence
