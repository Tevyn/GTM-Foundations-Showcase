# GTM Foundations -- Shared Context

## Company Overview
Ambient.ai is an AI-powered physical security and computer vision intelligence platform. Founded in 2017 (Y Combinator), named a Forbes Cloud 100 Rising Star. The platform includes four core products: Foundation (continuous monitoring), Advanced Forensics (AI-powered incident investigation), Access Intelligence (95% false alarm reduction via video/PACS correlation), and Threat Detection (150+ verified threat signatures). Also offers Pulsar, an edge-optimized Vision-Language Model. Targets enterprise physical security operations -- GSOC leaders, corporate security directors, and facilities executives. Integrates with existing camera and access control infrastructure (no rip-and-replace). Enterprise, sales-led, demo-driven motion with no public pricing. GDPR and SOC 2 Type II certified. Customers include Adobe, TikTok, ServiceNow, Cisco, MoMA, SentinelOne, Berkshire Hathaway Energy.

## ICP Context

- Target spans 8+ industries without prioritization: Education, Manufacturing, Energy & Utilities, Financial Services, Healthcare, Corporate Campuses, Data Centers, Museums
- Buyer could be physical security directors, VPs of corporate security, facilities leaders, IT executives, or campus safety directors depending on the vertical
- Pain points center on alert fatigue, slow forensic investigation, limited human attention on monitors, siloed video/access control systems, and difficulty proving security ROI
- Competitive landscape includes legacy VMS players (Genetec, Milestone, Avigilon), AI startups (Verkada, Rhombus), and the status quo of guards watching monitors
- No single dominant ROI narrative -- multiple proof points float without prioritization (95% false alarm reduction, 20x faster investigation, 10x faster incident response, headcount optimization)
- Company positions itself as overlay intelligence layer on existing infrastructure
- Enterprise motion with demo-driven sales suggests longer deal cycles and higher ACV
