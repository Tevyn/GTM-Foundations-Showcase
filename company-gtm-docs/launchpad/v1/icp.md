# LaunchPad — Ideal Customer Profile

## Target Market
**Industries:** Tech, Financial Services, Healthcare, Retail
**Company Size:** 50-5,000+ employees; $10M-$2B+ revenue
**Geography:** North America, EMEA

## Technographics
Currently using or managing multiple marketing tools (Marketo, HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, Google Ads) and manual spreadsheet-based campaign coordination; relying on email automation, ad platforms, and CRM separately without unified orchestration.

## Buying Triggers
- Looking to improve marketing campaign efficiency and visibility
- Need to centralize campaign management across fragmented tools
- Want to reduce manual coordination between marketing and sales teams
- Seeking better campaign performance visibility and reporting

## Buyer Roles
Marketing directors, VP of Marketing, marketing operations managers, demand generation leads, and marketing operations teams.

## Pain Points
- Manual coordination and handoff between multiple marketing tools creates delays and errors
- Campaign performance is scattered across different platforms, making reporting difficult and time-consuming
- Team members spend significant time building and managing UTM parameters manually across campaigns
- No unified view of campaign performance limits the ability to measure ROI and justify marketing spend
- Budget allocation across campaigns and channels requires manual analysis and guesswork
- Growing number of active campaigns outpaces the team's ability to manage them efficiently with current tools
- Integration gaps between marketing stack tools force workarounds and duplicate data entry

## Not a Fit
- Companies running single-tool marketing operations (HubSpot-only, Marketo-only) without integration pain
- Organizations without budget for platform subscription on top of existing tools
- Teams without dedicated marketing operations resources to drive adoption
