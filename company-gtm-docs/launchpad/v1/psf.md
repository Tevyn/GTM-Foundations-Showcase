# LaunchPad — Problem-Solution Overview

## VP Marketing / Director Level Perspective
**Priorities:** Campaign visibility across the entire martech stack, proving ROI on marketing spend, reducing manual reporting burden
**How LaunchPad Helps:** LaunchPad provides unified campaign orchestration and cross-platform analytics to give marketing leadership real-time visibility into campaign performance without jumping between tools.

## Marketing Operations Manager Perspective
**Priorities:** Streamlining campaign execution, eliminating manual coordination, managing UTM consistency
**How LaunchPad Helps:** LaunchPad automates campaign workflows and unified parameter management across marketing tools, reducing the operational overhead that comes with a fragmented martech stack.

## Demand Generation / Campaign Manager Perspective
**Priorities:** Faster campaign deployment, clearer performance tracking, coordinating across multiple channels
**How LaunchPad Helps:** LaunchPad centralizes campaign execution and provides real-time performance visibility, helping teams manage more campaigns with fewer coordination handoffs.

## Use Cases

### Campaign Orchestration & Unified Reporting [Flagship]
**Pain:** Marketing teams are managing campaigns across Marketo, HubSpot, Salesforce, and ad platforms simultaneously. Every campaign requires manually pulling performance data from each platform, building spreadsheets to compare results, and spending hours reconciling inconsistent metrics. Leaders can't see the real ROI of a campaign because performance is scattered — a lead from email might be tracked in Marketo, its value in Salesforce, and ad performance in Google Analytics. Friday afternoons disappear into reporting instead of strategy.

**Solution:** LaunchPad sits above the entire martech stack and orchestrates campaigns across all tools while collecting performance data in one place. Instead of jumping between platforms, marketing leaders see unified campaign performance, attribution across channels, and spend allocation in a single dashboard. Campaign managers can launch and adjust campaigns across multiple platforms without manual coordination or data entry.

### UTM Parameter & Campaign Configuration Management
**Pain:** UTM parameters are manually built and distributed across the team, leading to inconsistencies and wasted time.
**Solution:** LaunchPad's centralized campaign configuration builder and parameter management tools ensure consistency across all campaigns and channels.

### Budget Allocation & Performance Forecasting
**Pain:** Budget decisions across campaigns and channels are based on incomplete data and require manual analysis.
**Solution:** LaunchPad's unified reporting provides clear performance metrics per campaign and channel, supporting faster, more informed budget decisions.

### Campaign Scaling & Capacity Planning
**Pain:** Teams struggle to manage growing numbers of concurrent campaigns with current tools and processes.
**Solution:** LaunchPad's orchestration capabilities allow teams to manage more campaigns efficiently through automation and unified command center functionality.
