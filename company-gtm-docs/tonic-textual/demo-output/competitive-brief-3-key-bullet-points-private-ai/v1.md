# Competitive Brief: Private AI

## Overview

Private AI is a direct competitor in the PII/PHI detection space. They focus primarily on entity detection and redaction across text and documents. When a prospect is evaluating Private AI alongside Tonic Textual, here are the three key points to drive home.

---

## 3 Key Bullets for the Conversation

- **Synthesis vs. redaction-only.** Private AI focuses on PII detection and redaction, but lacks Tonic Textual's synthesis capabilities. Our platform doesn't just mask or delete sensitive data — it replaces PII/PHI with realistic synthetic alternatives (fake names, phone numbers, medical codes) that preserve semantic context and data structure. This matters because simple redaction breaks narrative flow and makes data less useful for AI model training and analytics. With Tonic Textual, teams maintain data utility while staying compliant with HIPAA, GDPR, and CCPA.

- **Audio redaction and synthesis.** Private AI is primarily a text-focused solution. Tonic Textual provides audio redaction and synthesis capabilities that enable organizations to process contact center recordings, patient conversations, and other audio sources. Commercial solutions for audio de-identification are sparse, and Tonic Textual is one of the few platforms that can detect spoken PII and replace it with realistic synthetic audio. If the prospect has any audio data use cases (call centers, healthcare recordings), this is a clear differentiator where Private AI cannot compete.

- **Real-time LLM privacy proxy and integrations.** As organizations adopt ChatGPT, Claude, and other LLM services, Tonic Textual provides a real-time privacy proxy layer that integrates directly with LangChain, Microsoft Fabric, and other orchestration frameworks. This means teams can submit sensitive prompts and context to LLM services without exposing PII — no rearchitecting needed. Private AI doesn't offer this level of native LLM workflow integration. For prospects building internal AI tools and chatbots, our platform provides privacy protection embedded directly in their LLM stack.

## Handling the Objection: "Private AI is cheaper / simpler"

If the prospect raises price or simplicity, redirect to data utility: "Private AI can detect and redact PII, but if your team needs to actually use that data for model training or LLM workflows, redacted data with [REDACTED] tokens breaks context and reduces model performance. Tonic Textual's synthesis approach means your data stays usable. The question isn't whether you can redact — it's whether your data is still valuable after you do."
