# Tonic.ai Textual — Messaging Guide

## Elevator Pitch

Tonic.ai Textual is an unstructured data privacy platform that uses proprietary NER models to detect and de-identify PII and PHI in text and audio. The platform preserves data context through realistic synthesis while maintaining compliance, enabling teams to unlock sensitive data for AI and analytics workflows.

---

## Value Propositions

### Unlock Sensitive Data for AI Without Compliance Risk
**Headline:** Use your real data for AI training and analytics without regulatory exposure.

**Supporting Copy:** Organizations have high-quality sensitive data (clinical notes, customer conversations, financial records) that would dramatically improve model performance, but compliance constraints prevent direct use. Tonic Textual detects PII/PHI and replaces it with realistic synthetic data, preserving context and utility. Teams can now train models on data that's representative of their domain while maintaining full regulatory compliance.

### Synthesis That Preserves Data Value
**Headline:** Maintain data utility for AI with realistic, synthetic data replacement.

**Supporting Copy:** Simple redaction (masking or deletion) breaks narrative flow and semantic relationships, making data less useful for model training. Tonic Textual's synthesis approach generates realistic replacements (fake names, phone numbers, medical codes) that maintain structure and context. Data scientists train better models because the data remains representative of their real-world problem.

### Production-Ready NER at Scale
**Headline:** Deploy enterprise-grade PII/PHI detection without building in-house NER pipelines.

**Supporting Copy:** Building and maintaining domain-specific NER models requires significant ML expertise and ongoing maintenance. Tonic Textual provides proprietary NER models tuned for PII/PHI detection across industries, with continuous updates for emerging data types. Teams get production-grade detection immediately, without years of engineering investment.

### Real-time Privacy Protection for LLM Services
**Headline:** Integrate privacy into LLM workflows without rearchitecting your applications.

**Supporting Copy:** As teams adopt ChatGPT, Claude, and other services internally, there's no easy way to protect sensitive data in real-time. Tonic Textual's privacy proxy integrates with LangChain and other frameworks, providing automatic redaction and synthesis. Sensitive data stays protected while teams gain access to the full power of modern LLM services.

## Notes

This messaging guide reflects Tonic Textual's broad positioning and multiple use cases. The value propositions are feature-oriented and don't clearly connect to a unified problem narrative or buyer journey. Different GTM functions would likely emphasize different value props depending on their audience (data science leadership vs. compliance leadership vs. platform engineering), and there's no clear guidance on which message to lead with or how to sequence them in a conversation. The brand voice is defined by adjectives rather than by actionable principles, making it difficult to apply consistently across channels. Sales teams would need additional talk track guidance to translate these value props into buyer-anchored conversations.
