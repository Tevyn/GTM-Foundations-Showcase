# PeopleGraph — Problem-Solution Overview

## Chief Human Resources Officer / VP of People Perspective
**Priorities:** Strategic visibility into workforce trends, reducing unplanned attrition, ensuring compliance and DEI narrative, controlling talent costs

**How PeopleGraph Helps:** PeopleGraph unifies workforce data from your existing HRIS, compensation, and recruiting systems into a single analytics layer. This gives CHROs and people leaders the predictive visibility and data foundation needed to make strategic workforce decisions — from identifying at-risk employees before they leave, to benchmarking compensation competitively, to tracking DEI progress against organizational goals.

## People Analytics Lead Perspective
**Priorities:** Reducing time spent on manual data assembly, faster reporting cycles, ability to model workforce scenarios, stronger analytics capability to support people strategy

**How PeopleGraph Helps:** PeopleGraph eliminates the need to pull data from multiple systems and stitch it together in spreadsheets. Pre-built workforce analytics models and dashboards let analytics teams deliver faster insights to stakeholders. Built-in scenario modeling for headcount and compensation planning reduces the manual forecasting burden.

## Manager and Line Leader Perspective
**Priorities:** Understanding team health and retention risk, compensation clarity, making data-informed decisions about their people

**How PeopleGraph Helps:** Managers get visibility into which of their direct reports show attrition risk signals, how their team's compensation stacks up competitively, and how their management practices compare to organizational benchmarks — enabling more informed conversations about retention and career development.

## Use Cases

### Identifying At-Risk Employees Before They Leave
**Pain:** HR teams discover attrition only after an employee resigns, leaving no time to intervene. Without a centralized view of attrition risk signals — recent performance reviews, internal mobility patterns, compensation gaps — leadership reacts to departures instead of preventing them.

**Solution:** PeopleGraph's predictive attrition model ingests historical HRIS and performance data to identify employees showing at-risk signals. HR teams can surface at-risk talent to managers proactively, triggering retention conversations before a resignation happens.

### Compensation Benchmarking and Pay Equity Analysis
**Pain:** Compensation teams lack a unified view of salaries across the organization. Building benchmark reports against market data requires manual pulls from multiple sources, and identifying pay equity gaps is time-consuming or impossible without an integrated dataset.

**Solution:** PeopleGraph aggregates compensation data from payroll and HRIS, then benchmarks it against market datasets and internal peer groups. Built-in pay equity analysis surfaces gaps and helps teams close disparities systematically.

### DEI Metrics Tracking and Reporting
**Pain:** Compiling diversity and inclusion metrics for board reporting or internal tracking involves assembling data from multiple systems. Without a central source of truth, data quality and consistency suffer.

**Solution:** PeopleGraph consolidates demographic and organizational data to create unified DEI dashboards, reducing reporting time and ensuring consistency across company communications.
