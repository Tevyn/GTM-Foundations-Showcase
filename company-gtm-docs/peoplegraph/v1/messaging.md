# PeopleGraph — Messaging Guide

## Elevator Pitch
"PeopleGraph is a people analytics platform that unifies your workforce data and delivers predictive insights — from attrition risk to compensation benchmarking to headcount planning — so HR leaders can make smarter, faster decisions about their people."

## Value Propositions

### Predict Attrition Before It Happens
**Headline:** AI-powered attrition prediction that identifies at-risk employees before they resign
**Supporting Copy:** PeopleGraph's predictive models analyze workforce data across your HRIS, performance, and compensation systems to flag employees showing early signs of flight risk. Instead of reacting to surprise resignations, HR teams get a 60-day early warning system that enables proactive retention conversations with managers — turning reactive backfill into proactive talent retention.

### Unified People Analytics Across Every System
**Headline:** One platform for all your workforce data — no more spreadsheet gymnastics
**Supporting Copy:** PeopleGraph connects to Workday, BambooHR, ADP, recruiting tools, and compensation systems to create a single source of truth for people data. Pre-built dashboards and analytics models replace the manual data pulls and spreadsheet stitching that eat up your analytics team's time.

### Smarter Compensation and Pay Equity
**Headline:** Benchmark compensation and close pay gaps with real-time data
**Supporting Copy:** See how every employee's compensation compares to market rates and internal peers. PeopleGraph surfaces pay equity gaps automatically, helping HR and finance make proactive adjustments instead of discovering disparities during exit interviews.

### Headcount Planning That Actually Works
**Headline:** Scenario-based headcount modeling for growing organizations
**Supporting Copy:** Move beyond spreadsheet-based forecasts. PeopleGraph lets HR and finance model headcount scenarios together — accounting for attrition, hiring velocity, and compensation changes — so your workforce plan keeps pace with your growth plan.

- **Empowering** — We put actionable insights in the hands of HR leaders and managers
