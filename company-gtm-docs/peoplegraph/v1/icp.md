# PeopleGraph — Ideal Customer Profile

## Target Market

**Industries:** Tech, professional services, manufacturing, retail

**Company Size:** 1,000–10,000+ employees

**Geography:** NA, EMEA, APAC

## Technographics

Currently using HRIS platforms (Workday, BambooHR, ADP) or manual HR processes (spreadsheets, separate tools for compensation and DEI tracking). May have fragmented people data across multiple systems with no integrated view.

## Buying Triggers

- Looking to improve visibility into workforce data and planning
- Need to reduce attrition or better predict at-risk employees
- Wanting to benchmark compensation across competitors
- Attempting to improve DEI metrics and reporting
- Scaling headcount and need planning clarity

## Buyer Roles

HR leaders (CHRO, VP of People), people analytics specialists, managers across engineering, operations, and business functions who touch workforce planning or compensation decisions.

## Pain Points

- **Manual workforce reporting:** HR teams spend time pulling data from multiple systems to build dashboards and reports
- **Lack of attrition visibility:** Can't predict which employees are at risk of leaving until they resign
- **Compensation inconsistency:** No centralized way to benchmark salaries or identify pay equity gaps
- **DEI tracking friction:** Reporting on diversity and inclusion metrics requires manual data assembly
- **Headcount planning complexity:** Building forecasts across departments without integrated models is time-consuming
- **Manager effectiveness blind spot:** No clear metrics for understanding how management quality affects retention

## Not a Fit

- Small teams (<1,000 employees) with simple, manual HR workflows
- Organizations without HRIS/HR technology stack
- Companies that don't value analytics or data-driven decision making
